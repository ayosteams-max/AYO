# Mission 25 — Tax and Regulatory Boundary

Pricing supports separately identified, versioned components for applicable VAT or
transaction tax, withholding, platform tax, airport/municipal charge and receipt/invoice
requirements. Each configuration records jurisdiction, legal basis reference, effective
dates, tax-inclusive/exclusive treatment, taxable base, rounding and approval evidence.

This architecture asserts no Ethiopian tax rate, classification or driver employment/
contractor treatment. Qualified Ethiopian tax and legal specialists must determine
applicability, invoicing, withholding, reporting, retention and cross-border/diaspora
obligations before numeric policy. Finance owns filing/reconciliation; Pricing applies
only approved configuration; Support and AI may explain published meaning but cannot
give legal conclusions.
