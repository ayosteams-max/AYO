# Mission 26 — Manual Adjustments, Accounting and Reporting

Manual adjustments require a typed reason, source case/evidence, currency, accounts,
amount, maker, independent checker, step-up authentication and idempotency key. They
create compensating journals; direct SQL balance edits and deletion are prohibited.
Material thresholds and privileged emergency procedure require Finance/Security approval.

Financial accounting consumes immutable subledger journals through versioned account
mappings and period controls. Operational event time, posting time and accounting
effective date remain distinct. Close, reopen and late-adjustment procedures require
Finance policy and complete audit.

Reports include trial balance, journal detail, wallet liabilities, driver payables, cash
receivables, provider clearing, refunds, chargebacks, commissions, incentives, taxes,
payout ageing, reconciliation exceptions and audit changes. Reports are reproducible by
as-of time/policy version, currency-separated, access-controlled and privacy-minimized.
Exports are evidence, not a second ledger.
