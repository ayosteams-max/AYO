# Mission 23 — Marketplace Health Metrics and Authority Boundary

Read-only metrics by approved city/zone/service/time bucket: median/tail rider wait,
driver idle, earnings-opportunity proxy, request/supply ratio, acceptance, cancellation,
redispatch, scheduled health, airport queue health, support cases, pickup failures,
weak-network impact, zone imbalance and reliability.

Snapshots carry definition/version, window, completeness, freshness, privacy threshold
and uncertainty. Sparse buckets suppress/coarsen. Typed recommendations require Operations
ownership. The engine cannot dispatch, price, bonus, suspend, restrict or expose an
individual’s performance.
