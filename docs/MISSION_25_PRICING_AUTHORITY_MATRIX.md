# Mission 25 — Pricing Authority Matrix

| Concern | Authority | May provide input | Prohibited boundary |
|---|---|---|---|
| Estimate/final/correction | Pricing | Maps, ride, airport, tax config | Client/AI cannot calculate authoritatively |
| Waiting/no-show eligibility evidence | Mission 20 | Notifications, pickup, disruptions | No fee or blame |
| Cancellation facts | Active Ride | Dispatch, Mission 20 | No financial consequence |
| Incentive programme/eligibility | Incentives | Marketplace Health advisory | No ledger posting; no hidden targets |
| Fare/commission/incentive amount | Pricing | Approved policy/entitlement | No money movement |
| Taxes/statutory component | Pricing from approved tax policy | Finance/legal | No engineering-invented treatment |
| Remedy authorization | Customer Recovery | Support investigation, Pricing recalculation | No direct balance mutation |
| Money movement | Wallet/Ledger | Idempotent settlement instruction | No mutable history |
| Payment collection/settlement | Payments | Licensed provider/cash evidence | No fare authorship |
| Cash confirmation | Reconciliation | Driver/rider/provider evidence | Driver tap alone is not proof |
| Investigation/explanation | Support | Role-scoped evidence | No fare calculation or money movement |
| Demand/health recommendation | Marketplace Health | Aggregate metrics | No price/incentive publication |
| Safety/restriction | Safety/Identity/Fraud | Anomaly evidence | Pricing cannot punish accounts |

Every cross-domain input is versioned, purpose-scoped and referenced rather than copied
where sensitive. AI is advisory only across all rows.
