# Mission 24 — Trusted Driver and Airport Eligibility Architecture

Trusted Driver is a versioned eligibility projection composed from active driver identity,
current documents, approved vehicle, service history thresholds, required training,
reverification, Safety/Eligibility result and explicit policy. It is not a popularity
score, reward ranking or permanent badge.

Airport Trusted Driver adds airport authority/access, terminal/zone training, vehicle/
service requirements and any locally approved language/accessibility capability. Airport
Standard and Premium remain separate. Missing/stale evidence yields ineligible or review,
never inferred trust. AI cannot grant or remove status. Drivers receive role-safe reasons,
expiry, corrective action and appeal; riders see only approved public attributes.
