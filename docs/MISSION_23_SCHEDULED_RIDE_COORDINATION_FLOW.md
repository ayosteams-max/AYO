# Mission 23 — Scheduled Ride Coordination Flow

`accepted -> soft plan -> health monitoring -> commitment -> confirmation -> activation/
pre-dispatch -> Active Ride handoff`.

Soft candidates may change only under deterministic material-improvement/stability rules.
After formal commitment, replacement requires driver withdrawal, eligibility/availability
loss, policy-defined lateness risk, conflict, airport closure or recovery timeout—not a
marginal score. Health includes confirmation, fresh work/location plan, current-trip risk,
airport/flight freshness, third-party consent/contact and fallback supply. Diaspora
booker, passenger and payer remain separate roles. Recovery is atomic and auditable.
