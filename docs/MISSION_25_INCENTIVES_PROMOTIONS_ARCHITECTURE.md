# Mission 25 — Incentives, Bonuses, Referrals and Promotions Architecture

Incentives is a separate deterministic authority for versioned programme rules and
eligibility. Pricing consumes an immutable entitlement snapshot; Wallet/Ledger later
executes an approved posting. Eligibility never proves payment.

Future programmes may cover airport supply, zone supply, Scheduled reliability,
completion quality, launch/referral, first trip and invited riders. Every programme has
purpose, cohort, geography, effective period, cap/budget, progress semantics, fatigue
override, anti-abuse rules, explanation and appeal. Hidden targets, retroactive changes,
coercive progress, speeding incentives and unsafe-hours encouragement are prohibited.

Referral rules support inviter/invitee benefits, period, campaign/geography, one-person-
one-benefit, device/account risk references, stacking rules, budget cap, cancellation
handling and diaspora-funded-family boundaries. Risk signals recommend hold/review;
protected traits and opaque device fingerprints cannot determine price. No marketing
automation or payment is authorized.
