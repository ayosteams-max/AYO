# Mission 24 — Privacy, Retention and Evidence Architecture

Apply purpose limitation, minimum necessary access, source separation, encryption,
case-level RBAC and access audit. Public projections never expose document numbers/images,
biometrics, raw contacts, precise device/network data, restricted mismatch reasons or
another participant’s evidence.

Retention categories: identity/account core; contact verification; authentication/session;
driver documents; vehicle evidence; biometric/selfie if separately approved; recovery;
fraud/safety reference; appeal; business/participant grant; immutable security audit.
Each requires purpose, lawful basis, owner, active/expiry retention, deletion/anonymisation,
legal hold and cross-border rule. Exact periods are unresolved Ethiopian legal decisions.

Deletion removes or irreversibly de-identifies eligible evidence but cannot falsify
required audit/legal records. Legal hold blocks deletion, not least-privilege access.
Production data is prohibited from AI training unless separately authorized; raw identity
documents and biometrics are prohibited from general-purpose AI.
