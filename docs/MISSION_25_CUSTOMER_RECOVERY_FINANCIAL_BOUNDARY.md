# Mission 25 — Customer Recovery Financial Boundary

Support creates/investigates a case and retrieves authorized evidence. Customer Recovery
may authorize an approved explanation, fare-correction request, partial/full refund
recommendation, next-ride-credit recommendation, apology or driver-compensation
recommendation. Pricing recalculates under the applicable policy when required.

Wallet/Ledger later executes an independently authorized, idempotent instruction. AI may
summarize, explain and recommend an allow-listed outcome only; it cannot authorize a
remedy, calculate a replacement fare, move money or establish legal blame. Material,
contradictory, repeated or fraud-suspected cases require authorized human/specialist
review. Every handoff preserves case, policy, evidence, missing facts and prior decision
references.
