# Mission 25 — Driver Earnings and Commission Architecture

The driver projection itemizes fare basis, time/distance, airport/premium, approved
waiting, incentive, toll/extra, commission, tax/statutory deduction, gross earning, net
earning and future wallet/cash status. Rider total and driver earnings are related
calculations, not falsely identical amounts.

Commission policies are immutable, versioned, effective-dated and scoped to approved
Standard, Scheduled, airport, promotion or temporary programme contexts. A lawful,
justified driver category may be used only after legal/fairness approval. The accepted
policy snapshot is retained; no change applies retroactively to a completed trip.

Sustainability dashboards use aggregate contribution margin, driver earnings per active
hour, affordability, cancellation/support/incentive/airport/payment costs, tax exposure
and fraud loss. They advise policy review and cannot rewrite a trip or publish a rate.
