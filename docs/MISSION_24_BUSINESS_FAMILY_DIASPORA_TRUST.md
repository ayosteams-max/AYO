# Mission 24 — Business, Family and Diaspora Trust

Business identities and people are distinct. Organization verification, administrators,
members, billing contacts, passengers and future payer authority use expiring role grants,
separation of duties and audit. An organization cannot recover a person’s account or view
unrelated rides merely because it pays.

Family/diaspora cases model booker, passenger, payer and representative independently.
Consent can be explicit, assisted or policy-approved for dependants/vulnerable users after
legal review. The passenger receives minimum trip/pickup safety information; the booker
receives bounded status; the payer receives only approved financial status. Revocation,
participant change and disputed authority are idempotent and appealable.
