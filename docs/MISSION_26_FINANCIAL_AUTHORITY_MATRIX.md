# Mission 26 — Financial Authority Matrix

| Concern | Exclusive authority | Inputs | Cannot do |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fare/commission/tax/incentive amount | Pricing | approved policies/evidence | move money |
| Remedy approval | Customer Recovery | Support case/Pricing recalculation | post ledger |
| Provider authorization/capture/refund/payout attempt | Payments | approved instruction | author fare; assert ledger balance |
| Financial movement | Ledger | validated instruction/provider/cash evidence | call provider; invent policy |
| Wallet balance/status | Wallet projection | posted ledger entries/holds | mutate independently |
| Cash claim | Cash workflow | rider/driver/ride evidence | prove settlement alone |
| Reconciliation | Finance Reconciliation | ledger/provider/bank/cash statements | rewrite history |
| Manual adjustment | Authorized Finance maker-checker | case and evidence | edit/delete posting |
| Dispute/chargeback investigation | Support/Payments specialist | scoped evidence | final legal conclusion |
| Fraud restriction | Fraud/authorized human | anomaly recommendation | AI auto-freeze/post |
| Accounting/reporting | Finance | immutable ledger and mappings | become operational ledger |
| AI | none | redacted authorized views | authorize or execute any transaction |

Separation of duties prevents one actor from drafting, approving and executing a material
adjustment, payout or policy publication.
