# Mission 24 — Testing and Evaluation Strategy

Test every identity/proofing/onboarding/recovery/appeal transition; idempotency, stale
versions, concurrency and out-of-order evidence; OTP expiry/resend/replay/enumeration;
refresh rotation/reuse, multi-device revoke, lost device and step-up; document duplicate/
tamper/expiry and driver-vehicle mismatch; Trusted/Airport eligibility composition;
business/family cross-role authorization; provider/database/audit outage; retention,
deletion/legal hold and cross-border denial; AI forbidden actions and prompt injection.

Use synthetic documents/faces/data only unless a separately approved representative,
consented, protected evaluation is authorized. Measure false accept/reject, manual-review
and appeal overturn, completion/abandonment, verification time, OTP delivery, recovery
takeover resistance, provider availability/cost, demographic/accessibility fairness and
Amharic/English comprehension. Numeric gates require Ethiopian baseline, legal review and
CTO/CEO approval. Any unauthorized approval/recovery, cross-account leak or audit loss is
zero tolerance.
