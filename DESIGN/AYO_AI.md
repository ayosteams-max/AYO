# AYO AI SYSTEM

## Mission

AYO AI exists to make every ride:

- Faster
- Safer
- Fairer
- Smarter
- More profitable for drivers
- Better for customers

AYO AI makes thousands of decisions every second.

---

# 1. Driver Matching

AYO AI chooses the best driver based on:

- Distance
- Traffic
- Driver Rating
- Acceptance Rate
- Cancellation Rate
- Driver Experience
- Vehicle Type
- Airport Priority
- Battery Level (future)
- Driver Availability

---

# 2. Traffic Prediction

AYO AI predicts:

- Heavy Traffic
- Road Closures
- Busy Events
- Rush Hour
- Airport Congestion
- Rain Delays

---

# 3. Smart Pricing

AYO AI calculates prices fairly.

It considers:

- Distance
- Time
- Traffic
- Demand
- Supply
- Airport Fees
- Toll Roads
- Promotions

AYO must never unfairly overcharge customers.

---

# 4. Fraud Detection

AYO AI detects:

- Fake Riders
- Fake Drivers
- GPS Spoofing
- Fake Locations
- Multiple Fake Accounts
- Payment Fraud
- Promo Abuse
- Account Sharing

---

# 5. Safety AI

AYO AI watches every trip.

It detects:

- Dangerous Stops
- Unexpected Route Changes
- Long Delays
- Driver Emergency
- Passenger Emergency
- Vehicle Standing Too Long

If needed:

- Notify Safety Team
- Contact Emergency Contacts
- Recommend Emergency Services

---

# 6. Smart Support

AYO AI answers common questions.

Examples:

- Where is my driver?
- Where is my payment?
- Lost Item
- Cancel Ride
- Update Destination
- Fare Question

If AI cannot solve the issue:

Transfer to a human support agent.

- Excellent Customer Service
