# AYO DRIVER MATCHING

## Goal

Find the best driver for every passenger.

Fast.

Fair.

Smart.

Reliable.

---

# Priority Order

1. Closest Driver

2. Driver Acceptance Rate

3. Driver Rating

4. Driver Safety Score

5. Vehicle Type

6. Driver Online Status

7. Driver Battery & Network

8. Driver Language (when needed)

9. Airport Queue Rules

10. AI Recommendation

---

# Driver Matching Rules

AYO should never choose only the nearest driver.

AYO should choose the driver most likely to provide the best experience.

---

# Smart AI Decisions

AYO AI considers:

- Traffic
- Road Closures
- Weather
- Driver Behaviour
- Passenger History
- Estimated Arrival Time
- Cancellation Risk
- Peak Demand

---

# Fairness

Every active driver should have an opportunity to receive trips.

The system must avoid giving all rides to the same drivers.

---

# Driver Rewards

Drivers with:

- High Ratings
- Low Cancellation
- Safe Driving
- Excellent Service

receive higher priority.

---

# Fraud Detection

Do not assign rides to:

- Fake GPS
- Fake Accounts
- Suspended Drivers
- Unsafe Drivers

---

# Airport Mode

Airport pickups use a separate queue system.

Premium Airport rides go only to verified drivers.

---

# Founder Promise

Every ride starts with one decision.

AYO will always make the smartest decision.
