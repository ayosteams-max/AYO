# AYO PRICING ENGINE

## Mission

Prices must be:

- Fair to passengers
- Fair to drivers
- Sustainable for AYO

Trust is more important than making extra money.

---

# Fare Calculation

Base Fare

+

Distance

+

Time

+

Traffic

+

Road Tolls (if any)

+

Airport Fee (if applicable)

=

Final Price

---

# Surge Pricing

AYO may increase prices only when:

- High demand
- Heavy rain
- Public holidays
- Major events
- Driver shortage

AYO must never abuse surge pricing.

---

# Driver Earnings

Driver earnings are calculated from:

- Base Fare
- Distance
- Time
- Tips
- Bonuses
- Incentives

Minus

- AYO Commission

Everything must be transparent.

---

# Passenger Discounts

- Promo Codes
- Referral Rewards
- Loyalty Rewards
- Student Discounts (Future)
- Senior Discounts (Future)

---

# Airport Pricing

Airport rides may include:

- Airport Access Fee
- Parking Fee
- Waiting Time
- Premium Airport Service

---

# Waiting Time

Passengers receive a free waiting period.

Extra waiting time is charged fairly.

---

# Cancellation Rules

Passenger Cancels

- Before driver arrives → No charge or small fee.

Driver Cancels

- Passenger pays nothing.

Repeated cancellations reduce account reputation.

---

# AI Pricing

AYO AI considers:

- Traffic
- Weather
- Demand
- Driver Supply
- Events
- Road Closures

The goal is always fair pricing.

---

# Transparency

Passengers always see:

- Total Fare
- Estimated Arrival
- Payment Method

Drivers always see:

- Earnings
- Bonus
- Commission

No hidden fees.

---

# Founder Promise

Fair pricing builds trust.

Trust builds customers.

Customers build AYO.
