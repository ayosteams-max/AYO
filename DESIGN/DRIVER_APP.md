# AYO DRIVER APP

## Purpose

Give drivers a fast, simple and secure way to receive trips, navigate, earn money and stay safe.

---

## 1. Driver Registration

- Full Name
- Phone Number
- National ID
- Driver Licence
- Profile Photo
- Emergency Contact
- Vehicle Details
- Bank or Mobile Money Details
- Terms and Conditions
- Background Check Consent

---

## 2. Driver Verification

- Identity Verification
- Licence Verification
- Vehicle Registration Verification
- Insurance Verification
- Profile Photo Match
- Phone Verification
- Fraud Check
- Manual Review When Needed

Only verified drivers can go online.

---

## 3. Driver Home Screen

- Online / Offline Button
- Current Location
- Daily Earnings
- Completed Trips
- Driver Rating
- Acceptance Rate
- Cancellation Rate
- Incentives
- Notifications

---

## 4. Incoming Ride Request

Show:

- Pickup Location
- Pickup Distance
- Estimated Pickup Time
- Trip Type
- Estimated Trip Distance
- Estimated Driver Earnings
- Payment Method
- Standard or Premium Ride
- Accept Button
- Decline Button
- Countdown Timer

AYO must not hide important information from drivers.

---

## 5. After Driver Accepts

- Rider First Name
- Rider Rating
- Pickup Pin
- Navigation to Pickup
- Call Passenger
- In-App Chat
- Arrived Button
- Wait Timer
- Cancel With Reason
- Safety Report

Phone numbers must remain private.

---

## 6. Passenger Pickup

Driver actions:

- Confirm Correct Passenger
- Start Trip
- Report No-Show
- Contact Support
- Report Safety Concern

The trip cannot start until the driver confirms pickup.

---

## 7. During Trip

- Live Navigation
- Destination
- Trip Progress
- Estimated Arrival Time
- Call Passenger
- In-App Chat
- SOS Emergency Button
- Share Trip With AYO Safety Team
- Report Route Problem
- End Trip Button

---

## 8. Trip Completion

- Final Fare
- Payment Status
- Driver Earnings
- Rate Passenger
- Report Problem
- Lost Item Report
- Trip Receipt
- Return to Online Mode

---

## 9. Driver Earnings

- Daily Earnings
- Weekly Earnings
- Trip-by-Trip Earnings
- Bonuses
- Incentives
- Tips
- Commission
- Deductions
- Cash Collected
- Mobile Money Payments
- Payout History

Every deduction must be transparent.

---

## 10. Driver Incentives

Possible rewards:

- Low Cancellation Bonus
- High Rating Bonus
- Completion Bonus
- Airport Trip Bonus
- Peak Time Bonus
- New Driver Bonus
- 50-Trip Challenge
- Safety Record Bonus

Bonuses must be fair, clear and achievable.

---

## 11. Driver Safety

- SOS Emergency Button
- Verified Passenger Indicator
- Trip Recording Option
- Live Trip Monitoring
- Emergency Contacts
- Block Passenger
- Report Passenger
- Fraud Detection
- GPS Spoofing Detection
- Fake Booking Detection
- 24/7 Safety Support

---

## 12. Driver Support

- Help Centre
- Live Chat
- Call Support
- Payment Dispute
- Trip Dispute
- Account Review
- Vehicle Change Request
- Document Renewal Reminder

---

## 13. Driver Standards

The Driver App must be:

- Fast
- Smooth
- Simple
- Secure
- Low Data Usage
- Low Battery Usage
- Easy to Use While Parked
- Clear in Bright Sunlight
- Reliable on Slow Internet
- Available in Multiple Languages

---

## 14. AYO Driver Promise

Drivers are partners, not just suppliers.

AYO must:

- Pay fairly
- Explain every deduction
- Avoid hidden rules
- Reward reliable drivers
- Protect drivers from fraud
- Listen to driver feedback
- Help drivers earn sustainably
