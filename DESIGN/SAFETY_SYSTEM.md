# AYO SAFETY SYSTEM

## Mission

Safety is the number one priority at AYO.

Every rider and every driver should feel safe before, during and after every trip.

---

# 1. Account Verification

Every account must verify:

- Phone Number
- Email (optional at launch)
- National ID where required
- Selfie Verification
- Device Verification

No fake accounts.

---

# 2. Driver Verification

Every driver must provide:

- Driver Licence
- Vehicle Registration
- Insurance
- Vehicle Photos
- Profile Photo
- Background Check (where available)

Only verified drivers can accept trips.

---

# 3. Passenger Verification

AYO may request extra verification for suspicious accounts.

Examples:

- Selfie Check
- SMS Verification
- ID Verification
- Payment Verification

---

# 4. Live Trip Tracking

Every trip includes:

- Live GPS Tracking
- Route Monitoring
- ETA Updates
- Trip Timeline

Mission 18's deterministic Active Ride Confidence Engine may identify developing
operational anomalies and request human safety review at approved thresholds. A
confidence level is not a safety conclusion, blame decision or permission to cancel;
missing/stale data lowers confidence, and the established safety authority decides any
intervention.

---

# 5. Emergency SOS

Both rider and driver can press SOS.

The app should:

- Share Live Location
- Alert AYO Safety Team
- Notify Emergency Contacts
- Suggest Emergency Services

---

# 6. Safe Communication

Passengers and drivers can:

- Call through AYO
- Chat through AYO

Real phone numbers remain hidden.

---

# 7. AI Safety Monitoring

AYO AI detects:

- Dangerous Route Changes
- Long Unexpected Stops
- Suspicious Behaviour
- GPS Spoofing
- Fake Trips
- Fraud Attempts

---

# 8. Trip Sharing

Passengers can share:

- Live Location
- Driver Name
- Vehicle Details
- ETA

with trusted family or friends.

---

# 9. Privacy

AYO protects:

- Phone Numbers
- Payment Details
- Home Address
- Personal Information

User data is encrypted and never sold.

---

# 10. Incident Reporting

Both riders and drivers can report:

- Unsafe Behaviour
- Harassment
- Violence
- Dangerous Driving
- Lost Items
- Fraud

Every report is reviewed.

---

# 11. Driver Safety

Drivers can:

- Block Problem Riders
- Report Unsafe Riders
- Receive Safety Warnings
- Request Support

---

# 12. Rider Safety

Passengers can:

- Block Drivers
- Report Drivers
- Request Help
- Cancel if They Feel Unsafe

---

# 13. Safety Principles

AYO will always:

- Put Safety Before Profit
- Protect Honest Drivers
- Protect Honest Riders
- Act Quickly
- Respect Privacy
- Follow the Law
- Continuously Improve

---

# Final Promise

Trust is earned every trip.

Safety is not a feature.

Safety is the foundation of AYO.
