# AYO NOTIFICATION SYSTEM

## Mission

Keep users informed at every step.

Notifications must be:

- Instant
- Useful
- Reliable
- Never annoying

# Smart AI Notifications

AYO AI should only notify users when helpful.

Examples:

- Leave now to avoid traffic.
- Demand is high in your area.
- Your usual route has delays.
- Airport demand is increasing.
- Your wallet balance is low.
